What is Oryx?

A simple guide to connecting your learning directly with your career goals.

Your One-Stop Platform for Growth

Tools for students, teachers, and mentors to learn, connect, and find jobs.

Create Your Profile

Quickly sign up to get your personal portfolio. Showcase your skills, track course progress, and make yourself visible to employers.

Find a Mentor

Connect with experienced mentors for career guidance. Or, if you're an expert, sign up to teach and guide many students at once.

Follow a Career Plan

Use our Career Roadmaps for step-by-step guides. Learn everything you need, from beginner to advanced skills, to succeed in your field.

Take or Teach Classes

Teachers can easily create classes for multiple students. Students can browse, filter, and join courses with a simple search.

Get Career & Job Help

  • AI Career Assistant: Get answers about careers and interviews from Michael, our AI bot.
  • Job Search Portal: Find jobs and internships from top sites like LinkedIn, all in one place.
  • Career Guidelines Hub: Access resume tips and helpful guides to boost your job hunt.

About Oryx

Our mission is to help people advance their careers through technology and community learning. Meet the team that built the platform on our "About Us" page.

“We chose the name Oryx because the oryx is a strong and smart animal with long, sharp horns. These horns help it protect itself and move forward with confidence. Just like that, our platform helps people grow their skills, stay strong in their journey, and move ahead in the right direction. The name ‘Oryx’ shows power, focus, and confidence — things we want every user to feel.”
